# Reshit - AI-Powered Jewish Digital Platform

**Reshit** is a modern AI-powered Jewish digital platform designed to make Jewish prayer, Torah learning, halacha, zmanim and community tools more accessible through a Hebrew-first, mobile-first user experience.

Reshit connects Jewish tradition with modern technology by combining artificial intelligence, digital Jewish content, elegant UI/UX, and practical tools for everyday Jewish life.

**Official website:** https://reshit.app  
**Founder:** Nevo Nadav  
**Category:** Jewish technology, AI platform, digital Judaism, Hebrew app

---

## What is Reshit?

Reshit is a Jewish app and digital platform built for people who want fast, clear and modern access to Jewish content and services.

The platform focuses on creating one central digital home for Jewish life - including prayer, Torah study, halachic information, Jewish calendar tools, zmanim, community features and AI-assisted learning.

Reshit is built with a strong focus on:

- Hebrew-first and RTL-first user experience
- Clean, modern and accessible interface design
- AI-powered Jewish content discovery
- Mobile-first usage
- Reliable access to Jewish knowledge and daily tools
- A bridge between traditional Jewish sources and modern digital habits

---

## Core Features

- **AI Jewish assistant** for learning, discovery and guided explanations
- **Smart siddur and prayer experience** with a modern reading interface
- **Jewish calendar and zmanim** based on location and daily Jewish times
- **Torah, Tanakh, Halacha and Talmud tools** for Jewish learning
- **Hebrew RTL interface** designed for native Hebrew usage
- **Personalized Jewish experience** based on user needs and preferences
- **Community tools** for Jewish events, insights and shared learning
- **Mobile-first digital product** built for practical daily use
- **Modern design system** with a clean and elegant Jewish brand identity

---

## Vision

Reshit was created to build a bridge between ancient Jewish tradition and the modern technological world.

The goal is to make Jewish knowledge, prayer and community services more accessible, understandable and useful through smart technology, high-quality design and AI-powered tools.

Reshit is not just another content app. It is a digital infrastructure project for modern Jewish life.
